# **App Name**: MediMate Rx

## Core Features:

- Multilingual AI Health Chatbot: Provides health information in multiple Indian languages with voice support. Acts as a tool providing verified information.
- Clinic & Resource Finder: Finds nearby clinics and provides real-time blood bank and medicine availability information.
- Blood Bank Management: Module for hospitals to manage blood inventory with real-time data on blood groups, units available, and expiry dates.
- Medicine Inventory Management: Tracks essential medicines and consumables in the hospital pharmacy, managing stock levels and expiry dates.
- AI-Powered Smart Dashboards: AI provides No-Show Prediction to optimize doctor schedules. Acts as a tool by processing and incorporating appointment data.
- Gamified Health Awareness: Quizzes and myth-busting content to improve health literacy.
- Appointment Booking: Schedule appointments with associated doctors directly through the platform.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A calming light-blue (#56ADE3) to reflect a medical environment.
- Background color: Off-white (#F5F7FA), very slightly tinted blue.
- Accent color: Teal (#34A7A7), to draw attention to important functions and CTAs.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', sans-serif, for a modern and clean user experience.
- Use clear and easily recognizable icons relevant to healthcare, ensuring they are distinct and accessible.
- Prioritize a clean and intuitive layout to facilitate easy navigation and access to critical information.
- Subtle animations to provide feedback and enhance user interaction without being distracting.
